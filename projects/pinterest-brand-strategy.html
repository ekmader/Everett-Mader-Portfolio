Pinterest Brand Strategy

27-slide narrative deck, mobile-friendly, interactive browsing experience.

This project is built as an interactive slide deck experience designed to let a viewer “flip through” a presentation without downloading the file. It preserves layout, typography, and quick transitions while keeping navigation in the browser.

Best format: PDF can be loaded in-browser with a renderer (PDF.js), but highest performance and design control comes from an image-based slide sequence (PNG/JPEG per slide). If you already have a PDF, upload it as projects/pinterest-brand-strategy.pdf and the viewer will stream pages directly.
