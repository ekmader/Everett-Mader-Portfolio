Design Film

A short film exploring design and perception.

This piece investigates how spatial narratives shape attention and memory. The film combines observational footage with interview excerpts to map experiential shifts in everyday settings.

Background

The film grew from a studio experiment in attention design, where simple interventions create meaningful changes in experience.

Approach

We foregrounded material detail and pacing to let viewers discover relationships between objects and place.
