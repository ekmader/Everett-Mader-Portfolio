Collapsible Shell Chair

An exercise in iteration and design logic. Exploring how simplicity can solve design constraints.

This collapsible plywood chair was designed with portability and simplicity in mind. Inspired by molded plywood forms, it functions as both a practical travel chair and an exploration of joinery without hardware.

Design Details

The shell chair showcases carefully considered joinery and plywood bending techniques. Each curve was designed to provide both structural integrity and visual elegance, creating a piece that's as beautiful as it is functional.

Design Process

The development of the collapsible shell chair involved extensive sketching and prototyping. Through iteration, I refined the form to balance portability with comfort, ensuring the chair could collapse flat while maintaining structural stability.
